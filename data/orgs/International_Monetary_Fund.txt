Name: International Monetary Fund (IMF)

Kind: International Economic Organization

Location: Worldwide (headquarters: Washington D.C., U.S.)

History:
    The IMF was formally founded in 1945 with 29 countries. Today there are over 188 countries that are involved in the organization. One of the primary reasons for creation of the IMF was in response to the Great Depression during the 1930's.
