Name: Human trafficking

Kind: Crimes against humanity

Location: Worldwide

Date and Time: Ongoing

Human Impact: 27 million people victims of human trafficking worldwide

Economic Impact: The human trafficking industry itself generates 32 billion USD, according to the International Labor Office, with about half of it (15.5 billion) being made in industrialized countries and a third of it (9.7 billion) made in Asia.

Resources Needed:

[1]: Donations
[2]: Human resources:
     [2.1]: Volunteers and activists
     [2.2]: Health care professionals (psychologists, social workers, etc.)
[3]: Global communication infrastructure (e.g.: hotlines, resource centers)

Ways to help:

[1]: At the local level, call available human trafficking resource centers to report human trafficking activity or to learn more about it by requesting training, technical assistance, or resources, if available.
[2]: Being informed about and reducing the use of material possessions (clothing, household goods, etc.) which may endorse and perpetuate human trafficking
[3]: Participate in or start a college organization to raise awareness of human trafficking
