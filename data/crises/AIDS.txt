Name: HIV-AIDS Epidemic

Kind: Disease

Location: global with an estimated 34 million cases as of 2010. Sub-Saharan 
	Africa is the most impacted. 

Date and Time: indefinite (ongoing)

Human Impact: Incurrable life threatening disease that has seen an exponential
	rate of increse in the number of cases since 1984.

Economic Impact: reduces the availability of human capital. People with AIDS grow sick and cannot work.

Resources Needed: N/A

Ways To Help: Donate to organizations such as The Global Fund and UNAIDS (links below)
