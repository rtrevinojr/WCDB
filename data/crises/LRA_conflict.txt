Name: Lord's Resistance Army

Kind: Military/Human Rights

Location: Uganda, South Sudan

Date and Time: 1987 - present

Human Impact: Notably, the biggest human impact the LRA is having is through their dealings with children.
	Since 1987, the LRA has abducted over 70,000 children. These children are then forced to fight for the 
	LRA or become sex slaves.

Economic Impact: N/A

Resources Needed: Simply put, more troops. This will come from the cooperation
	of different national forces including the US, Uganda, and the Central African Republic
