Human Trafficking

Ongoing
Crimes Against Humanity
People: Ricky Martin
Organizations: Polaris Project, Liberty Asia
  • Impact
    • 27 million people victims of human trafficking worldwide.
    • The human trafficking industry itself generates 32 billion USD, according to the International Labor Office, with about half of it (15.5 billion) being made in industrialized countries and a third of it (9.7 billion) made in Asia.
  • Resources Needed
    • Donations
    • Volunteers and activists
    • Health care professionals (psychologists, social workers, etc.)
    • Global communication infrastructure (e.g.: hotlines, resource centers)
  • Ways to help
    • At the local level, call available human trafficking resource centers to report human trafficking activity or to learn more about it by requesting training, technical assistance, or resources, if available.
    • Being informed about and reducing the use of material possessions (clothing, household goods, etc.) which may endorse and perpetuate human trafficking
    • Participate in or start a college organization to raise awareness of human trafficking
